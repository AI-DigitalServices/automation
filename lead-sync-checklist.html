The 5-Step Lead Sync Checklist

Follow these steps to diagnose your lead follow-up gaps and build an automated system

1

Diagnose Your Lead Black Hole

Find your last 10 leads from the past month. Use your CRM, spreadsheet, or inbox to track them.

✅ Success Metric

You can identify WHERE and WHEN you're losing leads.

2

Map Your 'Secret' Lead Workflow

Honestly answer how a lead currently moves from your website to a booked call. No judgment, just facts.

✅ Success Metric

You have a written, honest flowchart of your current process. Spoiler: If it lives in your head and inbox, it's broken.

3

Audit Your Tech Stack Communication

Check which connections are missing in your current setup. Be harsh with your evaluation.

✅ Success Metric

You know exactly which connections are missing. If you checked less than 3 boxes, you're manually bridging gaps that tech should handle.

4

Script Your 3-Must-Send Emails

You don't need 7 emails to start. Define these 3 core emails with subject lines and key value propositions.

✅ Success Metric

You have a simple, repeatable email sequence framework ready to automate.

5

Implement Your 'Booking Trap'

This is the most important step. Make it impossible for a warm lead to slip away by defining clear automation rules.

✅ Success Metric

You have a clear "if this, then that" rule for handling a booking, removing all manual work.

You've Completed the Checklist! What's Your Score?

Mostly Yeses: Your foundation is solid. You're ready to connect the dots and fully automate.

Mostly Nos: You're working too hard. The manual process is costing you clients. Automation will 10x your efficiency.
